Purchase & Accrue

As with today’s program, to accrue co-branding funds you will need to purchase specific processors from Intel and integrate them into your systems. Remember to save documentation related to processor integration rate for 18 months

Also, remember that purchases made after October 2nd, 2011 will accrue at the new rate of 2.5%

The new system tools will allow you to easily review:

  • Which processors accrue funds
  • Accrual balances
  • Any potential fund expirations (note: as with today’s program funds will expire after one year)
